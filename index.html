Welcome to Elfin International

We specialize in premium full-grain leather bags that blend luxury, durability, and style. From duffels to totes, each piece is a testament to our dedication to craftsmanship.

Our Legacy

Elfin International (LLP) is a premier manufacturer of full-grain leather bags, offering a perfect balance of luxury and affordability. We cater to clients worldwide with our export-quality products and bespoke customization services.

Our collections, including duffels, totes, slings, and laptop bags, are crafted to meet international standards while maintaining competitive pricing. Every piece reflects our passion for quality and innovation.

Choose Elfin International for leather goods that elevate your style and stand the test of time.

Meet Our Founders

Mehre Alam

Mehre Alam, a seasoned editor, author, and political commentator, began his journalism journey with Hindustan Times in 1995 and quickly rose to become Chief Copy Editor by 2001. With stints at top publications like Khaleej Times and Times of Oman, and impactful work at Oman Economic Review, his writing has spanned politics, business, cinema, and culture. A published author and known for his liberal voice, Mehre having ventured into the leather industry for above 5 years, bringing the same commitment to quality and depth with premium products.

Bahauddin

Bahauddin’s passion for innovation and design drives Elfin International’s unique offerings. He oversees customization and export operations, ensuring clients receive bespoke leather goods that reflect their personal style.
